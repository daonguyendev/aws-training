Final Instructor Assessment

Now that you have gone through the course, take this final instructor assessment to evaluate your understanding of the tool. There are 8 questions in total. You need to score 80% or higher to officially complete this instructor enablement course.

Question 1 of 8

Question
01/08

A student has registered with an email address different from their Amazon retail login. To correct this, the instructor should add the correct email to the roster in addition to the existing email. No further action is required for the student to obtain access. 


Based on the statement above, select True or False from the options below.

True

False

Incorrect

Correct answer: False


If a student’s email address is different to that which is on our system (as is likely given the requirement for Amazon Shopping logins), instructors must delete the previous email address before they can add another one.
