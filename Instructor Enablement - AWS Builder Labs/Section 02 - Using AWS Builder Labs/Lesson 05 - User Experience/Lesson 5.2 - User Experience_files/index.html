What class resources are now available to students in AWS Builder Labs?

  1. Front of card

    AWS Management Console

    Click to flip
    Back of card

    Once the lab is set to active by the instructor, students can start the lab and then open the AWS Management Console window in their lab environment to complete the lab tasks.

    Click to flip
  2. Front of card

    Student & Lab Guide

    Click to flip
    Back of card

    Students can now access their student and lab guides in eVantage Bookshelf (VitalSource) by selecting the buttons for each on their AWS Builder Labs landing page. They will be redirected to the VitalSource log in page where they can enter their credentials. They can also skip log in and view the guides, but this is not recommended since it doesn't associate the student with their license key which means they will not be able to access guides after the class ends. Always encourage students to log in to their eVantage Bookshelf (VitalSource) account to access the guides.


    (Important note: If students log into VitalSource, guides are automatically added to their browser based bookshelf with a 2 year expiry. Ask students to click on the download option during class to retain persistent access to the guide on their local machine.)

    Click to flip
  3. Front of card

    Virtual Classroom Link

    Click to flip
    Back of card

    Students will have the link to their virtual classroom (Webex, GoToMeeting, Zoom, etc.) available from the AWS Builder Labs landing page if it was added into the class location field during the class setup by the instructor. Alternately, some instructors are choosing to add the www.aws.training(opens in a new tab) class URL to the class location field so that students can access their transcript and survey easily at the end of class. If using the latter option, the virtual classroom (Webex, GoToMeeting, Zoom, etc.) URL will also be listed on the AWS Training site for students to access.

    Click to flip
